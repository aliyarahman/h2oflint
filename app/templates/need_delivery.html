Dear Flint Resident,

We realize that many of our neighbors are unable to travel to various locations around the city in search of water. Until this crisis is over, we want to make sure all of our residents are receiving water and as much as they need.

We soon will be able to provide on a regular basis a mobile-water at-home delivery service for our seniors and others most challenged by this crisis. Once the system is ready for service an announcement will be posted on this site.

Until then, please let us know if there is anything else we can do to be helpful to you during this time of unprecedented challenge.
